History

TechCon began as a small gathering of innovators and has grown into a premier technology conference. Over the years, it has expanded globally, fostering collaboration and innovation in multiple industries.

Mission

Our mission is to empower technologists, entrepreneurs, and leaders to drive positive change through innovation, knowledge sharing, and collaboration.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Recognized as the world’s first computer programmer, Dr. Ada Lovelace inspired generations of innovators with her groundbreaking ideas on computing.

Alan Turing

A mathematician and pioneer of artificial intelligence, Alan Turing’s contributions laid the foundation for modern computing.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper, a U.S. Navy rear admiral and computer scientist, developed the first compiler and was instrumental in the creation of COBOL.
